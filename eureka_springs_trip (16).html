Sample Itinerary

July 16 – Arrival

Check into your charming lodging and enjoy a peaceful evening stroll through historic downtown. Consider staying at:

  • Treehouse-Style Cabin with Hot Tub
  • Romantic Studio Overlooking Nature
  • Historic Downtown Loft
  • Charming Cottage Near the Springs
  • Secluded Retreat in the Woods

July 17 – Historic Tour

Visit the Eureka Springs Historical Museum, explore the preserved Victorian architecture, and ride the historic tram tour.

July 18 – Art Day

Explore art galleries and studios. Visit Zarks Fine Design Gallery, Eureka Springs School of the Arts, Eureka Springs Festival of the Arts, The Heart Division, and Wilson and Wilson Folk Art Gallery.

July 19 – Nature & Gem Mining

Go hiking on nearby trails and picnic by the springs. In the afternoon, visit a local gem mine. Check out Quigley's Castle, Wegner Quartz Crystal Mines, War Eagle Cavern, and Dinosaur World / Ozark Mineral Museum.

July 20 – Relax & Recharge

 Visit Suchness Spa, Spa 1905, or New Moon Spa for full-body rejuvenation.

July 21 – Scenic Drive

Take a scenic drive through the Ozarks. Stop at viewpoints and local farms. Explore Thorncrown Chapel and its stunning forest setting.

July 22 – Final Explorations

Revisit favorite places or explore anything you missed. Cap off the trip with a sunset dinner together.

July 23 – Departure

Pack up, grab a farewell breakfast, and head home with beautiful memories.
