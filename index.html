Sample Itinerary

July 16 – Arrival

Check into a charming Victorian inn. Stroll through downtown and enjoy a welcome dinner at a vegan-friendly café.

July 17 – Historic Tour

Visit the Eureka Springs Historical Museum and take a walking tour of local architecture. End the day with a ghost tour!

July 18 – Art Day

Explore art galleries and studios. Attend a local art class or workshop. Catch a live performance in the evening.

July 19 – Nature & Gem Mining

Go hiking on nearby trails and picnic by the springs. In the afternoon, visit a local gem mine like Blue Ribbon Gem Mining to discover quartz, amethyst, and other treasures.

July 20 – Relax & Recharge

Enjoy a spa day or hot tub soak. Browse the quirky shops downtown. Have a slow dinner at a romantic spot.

July 21 – Scenic Drive

Take a scenic drive through the Ozarks. Stop at viewpoints and local farms. Explore Thorncrown Chapel.

July 22 – Final Explorations

Revisit favorite places or check out anything you missed. Enjoy a sunset dinner together.

July 23 – Departure

Pack up, grab a farewell breakfast, and head home with memories to treasure.

Romantic Things to Do

  • Horse-drawn carriage rides through the historic district
  • Watch the sunset from the Crescent Hotel gardens
  • Picnic at Lake Leatherwood under shaded trees
  • Couples massage at a local spa
  • Write a love note and tuck it into a hidden spot along a nature trail

Events in Late July

During your stay, check out local events that celebrate summer and creativity:

  • Eureka Springs Farmers Market (Thursdays) – Fresh produce and handmade goods
  • Drumming in the Park (Weekends) – Join or watch local drummers in Basin Spring Park
  • Evening Concert Series – Outdoor music events downtown
